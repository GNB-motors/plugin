SCREENSHOT + PROMO TILE — fastest paths
========================================

Required for Chrome Web Store submission:
  • 1 screenshot at 1280×800 (PNG or JPEG)
  • 1 small promo tile at 440×280 (PNG or JPEG)

----------------------------------------
SCREENSHOT (1280×800)
----------------------------------------

OPTION A — capture the real popup, then frame it
  1. Load the unpacked dist/ in chrome://extensions
  2. Open the popup, sign in, get to the main view
  3. Press Ctrl+Shift+I in the popup, then Ctrl+Shift+P
     → "Capture node screenshot" on the <body> element
  4. Open the resulting PNG in Paint.NET / GIMP / Figma / Canva
  5. Create a 1280×800 canvas with a solid color background
     (suggested: #1e40af or #0f172a)
  6. Center the popup screenshot, add a 1-line caption like
     "One-click fleet telematics audits"
  7. Export as PNG.

OPTION B — Canva template (fastest)
  1. canva.com → search "Chrome extension screenshot"
  2. Pick any 1280×800 template
  3. Replace the placeholder image with your popup screenshot
  4. Replace the headline text with: "gnbedge — fleet fuel audits"
  5. Download as PNG.

----------------------------------------
PROMO TILE (440×280)
----------------------------------------

OPTION A — Canva
  1. Custom size 440×280
  2. Background: solid #1e40af
  3. Place icon128.png (or a 200px version) on the left
  4. Right side text:
       Title: gnbedge
       Tagline: Fleet fuel audits, automated.
  5. Download as PNG.

OPTION B — Figma
  1. New frame 440×280, fill #1e40af
  2. Drop in icon128.png at 180×180, left-aligned
  3. Text frame on right with title + tagline (above)
  4. Export as PNG.

----------------------------------------
CHEAT: even simpler
----------------------------------------
A screenshot of the popup pasted onto a colored 1280×800 canvas
with no text at all is enough to pass review. The promo tile can
be just the icon centered on a solid background.

Save both PNGs anywhere — you upload them in the dashboard form,
they don't need to live in the repo.
